# No Trolls In Raids

RuneLite plugin that highlights players from your OSRS Ignore List inside ToB and ToA party boards.

Ignored players are automatically turned red so you can quickly spot trolls, griefers, or players you want to avoid before joining a raid.

## Features

- Works in Theatre of Blood and Tombs of Amascut
- Detects players from your Ignore List
- Turns ignored names red on raid boards and party lists
- Lightweight and automatic

## Usage

1. Add players to your OSRS Ignore List
2. Open ToB or ToA party board
3. Ignored players will appear in red
